Research

Larval connectivity

I use oceanographic particle-tracking models to investigate seasonal and interannual variation in coastal connectivity.

Biogeographic boundaries

I examine how transport barriers may contribute to community turnover and species-range boundaries.

Community ecology

I integrate visual surveys, environmental DNA, and climate data to study changes in marine communities.
